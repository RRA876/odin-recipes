How to make tasty aaloo bhujia

Description :

In this page we learn to make tasty aaloo bhujia you will love preparing the recipe

Ingredients :

  • long chopped aaloo(potato) as you see in the picture
  • haldi
  • garam masala
  • namak according to your taste

steps to prepare swadist aaloo bhujia :

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds / jeera (optional), let them splutter.
  3. Add haldi, chili powder, and a pinch of hing (if using).
  4. Add diced potatoes (boiled or raw).
  5. Add salt and garam masala.
  6. Mix well and cook on medium flame, stirring occasionally.
  7. Cook till potatoes are soft and edges slightly crispy.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander.
  9. Serve hot with rice or roti.
