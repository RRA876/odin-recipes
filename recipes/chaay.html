How to make tasty chaay

Description

chaay is very famous drink in whole india.We will learn how to prepare tasty chaay

Ingredients :

  • Water 1 cup
  • Milk ½ cup
  • Tea leaves 1 to 1½ tsp
  • Sugar 1 to 2 tsp (as per taste)
  • Cardamom or Ginger 1 crushed (optional but tasty)

How to prepare

  1. Boil 1 cup water in a pan.
  2. Add crushed cardamom or ginger.
  3. Add tea leaves and boil for 1-2 mins.
  4. Add milk and sugar.
  5. Simmer for 2-3 mins until color deepens.
  6. Strain and serve hot!
