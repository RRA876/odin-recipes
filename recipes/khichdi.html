How to make delicious khichdi !

Description

In this page we will learn how to make delicios khichdi at home

Ingredients :

  • Rice
  • Dal
  • Some vegetables
  • Spices
    • termeric
    • garam masala
    • sabji masala
  • salt as per your taste
  • water

steps to prepare delicios khichdi :

  1. first of all add water in a dish and leave it to boil.
  2. then add rice and dal in 2:1 ratio.
  3. also add termeric powder and some salt into it.
  4. take another dish and fry the chopped vegetables with sabji masala and garam masal (Note: dont forget to add garam masala as it wil, enhance the taste of your dish).
  5. after the vegetables are fried, add it to the ric and dal and leave it to cook properly.
  6. serve and enjoy with your family.
